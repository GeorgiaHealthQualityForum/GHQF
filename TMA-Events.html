Past Events

MEDIC

Date: November 26 - 28, 2024

The "Medicine: Innovations and Challenges 2024" conference is held in a hybrid form and addresses current issues in medicine and healthcare, presenting innovative and interdisciplinary research and their outcomes by international speakers. Patient safety and patient centered care themes are parts of agenda and presented by speakers from Brandenburg University: “Understanding of AI bias – the implications for patient safety”, “CyberPatient – the approach of innovations and challenges”, “HER – Germany experience”, “Evaluation of local large language models for the development of patient safety in oncology”.

World Patient Safety Day

Date: September 17, 2024

What should future doctors know about international patient safety requirements?

TMA recently hosted a panel discussion on this topic, where specialists and students engaged in a relevant dialogue.

Patient Safety Student Conference

Date: May 27, 2024

This highly interactive conference aimed to raise awareness about patient safety issues in current clinical practice and generate student understanding on international standards of patient safety.

After a welcome from the Dean, Prof. Lali Mekokishvili, four physicians presented patient safety cases and moderated interactive discussions after each. Students also shared, analyzed and reflected on their own experience.
