Collaborators

The Georgia Health Quality Forum is a long-term project to reduce patient harm by building a professional capacity in quality improvement and patient safety. Our team brings together clinicians and researchers from across the world who want to make a difference in Georgian healthcare. We hope that you will find our work valuable and consider joining us.
