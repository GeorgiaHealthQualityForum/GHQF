The Team

The Patient Safety Initiative of Georgia is part of a broader effort sponsored by the Fullbright Program to elevate patient safety standards and improve healthcare infrastructure. Our team is led by Peter F. Martelli, PhD, MSPH, and leverages many years of combined experience in research and healthcare reform to serve the medical community. We hope that you will find our repertoire of curated content in patient safety useful and join us on this journey to a safer future for Georgia.

Core Members

Peter F. Martelli, PhD, MSPH

Peter F. Martelli is an Associate Professor of Healthcare Administration in the Sawyer Business School at Suffolk University. His research focuses on system safety and reliability. He holds a PhD from the University of California - Berkeley and was a Fulbright Scholar to Georgia from 2021-23.

Eka Rukhadze, MD, PhD

Eka Rukhadze is co-Head of the Department of Professional Development at Georgian American University, and Head of Quality and Patient Safety Department at Curatio Clinic in Tbilisi. She holds a PhD from David Tvildiani Medical University.

Shagorika Dé, MD, MHA

Shagorika Dé is a highly motivated and accomplished healthcare professional with a passion for improving patient care and operational efficiency within the healthcare industry.
