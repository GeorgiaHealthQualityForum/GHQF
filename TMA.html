TBILISI MEDICAL ACADEMY

The Patient Safety Initiative of Georgia is part of a broader effort sponsored by the Fullbright Program to elevate patient safety standards and improve healthcare infrastructure. Our team is led by Peter F. Martelli, PhD, MSPH, and leverages many years of combined experience in research and healthcare reform to serve the medical community. We hope that you will find our repertoire of curated content in patient safety useful and join us on this journey to a safer future for Georgia.
