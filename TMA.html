Patient Safety Student Conference, 27 May 2024
      This highly interactive conference aimed to raise awareness about patient safety issues in current clinical practice and generate student understanding on international standards of patient safety.
      After a welcome from the Dean, Prof. Lali Mekokishvili, four physicians presented patient safety cases and moderated interactive discussions after each. Students also shared, analzyed, and reflected on their own experience.
